Swimming Pool

Tyndale Biscoe School was the first school in the Kashmir Valley to introduce the idea of swimming pools. This being the school map will not discuss its history and let's have a look on its location.

The swimming pool is located is located in the Junior Department of the School but is common to every department of both schools (Mallinson School only upto class 5th.). It is located opposite to the main play field of the Biscoe campus.
